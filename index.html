About JBK SEARCH

JBK Search is a retained executive search firm whose primary aim is to help companies thrive and succeed by assisting them in attracting, identifying, hiring, and retaining the best people.

One thing our company will continue to strive to achieve is raising money for organizations that continously fuel our passion for recruitment.

We were founded in 1999 by a recruiting industry veteran with a vision to create a search firm that would help companies help patients live longer and healthier lives because of the candidates we place.

On a partnered and engaged basis, JBK Search has been helping pharmaceutical and biotech companies attract, recruit, vet and hire bright talent since 1999.
