The Recruitment Of Possibility

We are experienced recruiters. We are trusted advisors.
Our clients seek us out because of our industry experience and straightforward, no-nonsense approach to recruiting.

JBK Search is a global recruiting firm committed to connecting top talent with passion-driven organizations in the biotechnology, biopharma, molecular diagnostics, medical device, medical software, laboratory services, and nonprofit industries.

  • We ask many questions during the initial assessment
  • We create a profile of the candidate based on the skills needed and the personality desired
  • We assemble an exhaustive list of candidates from our database of over 12,000 candidates, our extensive network, and our state-of-the-art search tools
  • We conduct an initial interview and assessment, followed up by our Founder & CEO’s proprietary behavioral-based interview
  • We present the candidate to the leadership team
  • We coach the client on areas to dig in and the candidate’s hot buttons
  • We cover the counteroffer (early & often)
  • We discuss any concerns or needs with the candidate’s significant others or family if needed concerning issues such as relocation
  • We negotiate offers
  • We help with onboarding if asked
  • We stay in touch with the candidate and client after the placement to address any concerns

Questions? Send us an email or give us a call!
